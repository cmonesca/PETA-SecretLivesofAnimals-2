This book has a purpose:

To show you how animals really live and sound today.

But it also has a goal: to make you realize that stopping all this cruelty is up to you.

Not wearing fur. Not going to animal shows. Never using a product tested on animals. Not hunting them, not caging them and, of course, not eating them.
